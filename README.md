# Wayfair AI Agent Engineering for Business Intelligence

<b>Status:</b> 🏗️ In-Progress (Live Sessions: March 9 – May 4, 2026) 

This repository documents my 8-week externship with Wayfair, focusing on building AI agents that automate the transition from raw market data to actionable business strategy

----


## 🎯 Externship Milestones:

- <b>Project 1:</b> Moodboard Agent – Transforming style prompts into curated visual outputs. 


- <b>Project 2:</b> Market Trend Discovery – Building agents to scrape and analyze product data and social media. 

- <b>Project 3:</b> Competitor Benchmarking – Tracking competitor launches and pricing to identify "whitespace" opportunities. 

- <b>Project 4:</b> AI Insight Generation – Using Gemini to turn raw signals into marketing content and campaign ideas. 


- <b>Project 5:</b> Market Intelligence Dashboard – Designing a live, auto-updating Google Sheets tool for supply chain managers.


## Key Technical Details

- <b>Automation Engine:</b> n8n 

- <b>LLM:</b> Google Gemini 

- <b>Hardware Baseline:</b> 8GB RAM / Intel i3 or above 

- <b>Core Skills:</b> Data Scraping, Prompt Engineering, and Dashboard Design
